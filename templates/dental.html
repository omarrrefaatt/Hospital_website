Dental Center

Top Dentists In Egypt

The dental department treats many dental diseases, the most famous of which are tooth decay, tooth sensitivity, gingivitis, tooth discoloration, tooth erosion, excessive number of teeth, dental holes and pits, and pus around the teeth, in addition to some oral diseases such as dry mouth, bad breath, And Kaposi's sarcoma, in addition to diseases of the oral mucous membranes.
